Ships and Aircraft Training Help

The "Ships and Aircraft Training" App is used as a Naval training aid. Two methods can be used to navigate the card deck, you can use the on-screen buttons, or touch-swipe gestures.

Tap to flip

Swipe to change

Card Difficulty

If you mark a card as "Easy", it will appear less often in the deck than "New" cards or cards marked as "Tough".
